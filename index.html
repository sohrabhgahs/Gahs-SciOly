Glen Allen HS Science Olympiad

Compete. Discover. Lead.

Science Olympiad is a premier national STEM competition featuring 23 dynamic events spanning earth science, biology, chemistry, engineering, and building challenges. Teams of up to 15 students compete at regional, state, and national levels.

Join the Team

About Our Team

How We Operate

The Glen Allen High School Science Olympiad (**Division C**) team is comprised of up to 15 dedicated students. We tackle 23 diverse events across various scientific disciplines, featuring a strategic mix of study-based exams, laboratory practicals, and engineering build events.

Students are selected based on their specific strengths and interests. Science Olympiad is nationally recognized and operated by soinc.org.
